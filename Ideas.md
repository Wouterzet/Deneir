
**Pneumatic tube network** (Windpost)
The Halruaan used a pneumatic pipe network to send around messages on scrolls in tubes.
The access points for these are bolt action slots in the wall with a hole about 4 centimeters in diameter. The way to use these would be to flick a lever to the left and down to open up a slot where one would place the message tube. By then moving the lever up and right you would close and lock the slot. By then placing the palm of your hand on the opening and using gust you can propel the tube along the pipe network.

The Halruaan people would send these messages back and forth after adding a response. The capsules contain a long scroll that has replies to messages.

**Entering the Deneir Project**
The Deneir shrine where the players get linked to the pillars is also where they get teleported to the Deneir project underground. To get teleported there they need to go to [[The Deep Current Station]] and reroute power to the teleporter. Power has been cut off from there to conserve energy.
