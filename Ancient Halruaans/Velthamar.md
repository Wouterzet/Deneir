Velthamar Velthorn, [[Halruaan]] (He/Him), married to [[Vaerys]]. Deceased

Velthamar carried on his wife's work in her name and began experimentation on the portals. He discovered how to enter the exit portal's dimensional coordinates to target where it opened. He was the first Halruaan to start to chart out the dimensional coordinates of our current and our original world. 

While experimenting with both the entrance and exit in the same dimension the Halruaan discovered something strange: even though the portal that was created was nothing different from a regular doorway, objects and signals appeared to come through to the other side slightly before they entered. 

This negative time interval between an object arriving and departing was incredibly miniscule (roughly one hundred-thousandth of a second). The Halruaan were skeptical if their equipment could even measure time to such a small degree.
