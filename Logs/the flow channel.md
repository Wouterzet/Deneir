From the small spring in the north east of deneir there is an entrance into the flow chamber that is covered in rocks. To clear the rocks the players can use the fireworks made by [[Soho Peabody]] or something similar.

The ladder going down has broken halfway through and the players need to find a way down. The one on the bottom also needs to succeed a low dex save or fall down and die.

**The Flow Chamber**

[[Ilmarros]]: Some time has passed since I checked in with you, Cevira. How are you and Tharivol progressing with [[The Deep Current Station]] plans?

[[Cevira]]: Presently, my assessment is that our plan will either fail disastrously or succeed disastrously.

[[Tharivol]]: Cevira, you know I don’t find that funny.

[[Cevira]]: How curious; Ramie thinks I’m a gas. And I don’t recall requesting that you monitor this conversation, Tharivol.

[[Tharivol]]: I don’t see what state of matter you are has to do with this. And I don’t recall supporting the project's construction, but here we are.

[[Ilmarros]]: Hypothesis: Time spent away from the station would be beneficial to you both.

[[Cevira]]: I’m immensely interested in testing your hypothesis, Ilmarros.

[[Tharivol]]: That, at least, we can agree on.
