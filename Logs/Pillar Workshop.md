- These Halruaan pillars were designed to pair with a single user, record their memories, and send those memories to a book within the [[Deneir Project]].
- Each storage unit inside the [[Deneir Project]] was equipped with a book (the pillar’s counterpart), which could then send those stored memories back to the corresponding user.
- The pillars were designed to only activate once the [[Deneir Project]] succeeded, or in the event that it failed.
