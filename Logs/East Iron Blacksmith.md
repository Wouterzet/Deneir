This is [[Deneir Village]]'s blacksmith. Most of the stuff made here are farming tools or household items. [[Relfrego Ironmaw]] has made some weapons but they are not particularly high quality and seem to mostly be to make the Blacksmith seem genuine. 

At the front entrance the players can find a stone slab half in the ground that has some Halruaan written words on it.

Translated it says: "[[Pillar Workshop]]"

In the basement of the blacksmith the players can find a pillar between the boxes and barrels that looks like one of the pillars in the [[shrine]]. Upon further inspection it looks unfinished. 

On the wall they can find a windpost station with a windpost capsule stuck inside. The capsule has the following message:

**Fail-safe explanation**

[[Ylindra]]: I’ve installed the books inside the [[Deneir Project]], [[Cyrandel]]. They look beautiful.

[[Ylindra]]: It’s comforting to know the pillars will not pair until the project succeeds. Otherwise, I imagine the experience would be hard to endure!

[[Cyrandel]]: Ideally, they’ll only need to activate once the project succeeds; as a safety measure, however, the pillars will also activate in the event of equipment failure.

[[Ylindra]]: They will? Why is that?

[[Cyrandel]]: If anything goes wrong with the Deneir Project, the pillars (and their books) will make us aware of the situation and enable us to fix it. Otherwise, it would be possible for us to remain permanently unaware of the problem.

[[Ylindra]]: I hadn’t thought of that! What a profoundly horrific fate that would be.



If the players ask Relfrego or Percy about these capsules they might tell the players about another one that they thought belonged to their wife/mother. They haven't had the strength to go through her stuff but will grab a box with the capsule in it when asked about it. The capsule has the following message:

**Memory pillar recording**

[[Ilmarros]]: [[Cyrandel]], how are things proceeding with the pillars?

[[Cyrandel]]: The first test with pairing the pillar to [[Zeruvan]] has gone smoothly. The eye opened and the flame ignited. Now, no matter where he is, [[Zeruvan]]’s pillar will record his memories and send them to the Deneir Project.

[[Zeruvan]]: He has outdone himself again with the sculpting work as well I might add! And now that we have our first successful pairing, we can test my memory storage prototype.

[[Zeruvan]]: Each pillar will send a single Halruaan’s memories to his or her own storage unit within the project.

[[Cyrandel]]: Each storage unit will be equipped with a book, the pillar’s counterpart, which will be able to send those stored memories back to the corresponding Halruaan.
