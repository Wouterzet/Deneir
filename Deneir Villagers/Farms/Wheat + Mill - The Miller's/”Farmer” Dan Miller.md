Dan Miller, being a part of the longest existing family in Toki, is also highly regarded by most folk in the town, who often come to him in times of need.  It is “Farmer” Dan who came up with the idea that everyone in the town should repeat the same actions every day.  While doing this he has asked them to find one moment each day to appreciate that was a little different from the last.

He also has 2 daughters: [[Anya Miller]] and [[Maybell Miller]]
